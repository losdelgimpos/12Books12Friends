How it works

Ask 12 friends to nominate

Tell them to choose something personally tailored for you which they think you should read (and you haven't read before)

Your friend selects a book

Following 3 simple rules - your friend must have read the book, it must be a single volume and it cannot be a textbook

You read the book

You've got 12 months to read the books - and don't forget to write a thank you note and tell your friend what you thought when you finish
