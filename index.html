Let your friends choose what you read

12 books | 12 friends started as a new year's resolution.
12 close friends choose a life-changing book for me to read within 12 months. Now I invite you to take the challenge yourself.

How it works

Ask 12 friends to nominate a book

Carte blanche for them to choose whatever they think would be best for you

They choose a book which meets the rules

No textbooks, single volumes only and they've got to have read it themselves.

You read the book within 12 months

Enjoy the book, learn something new and don't forget to write a thank you note
